STEVIA TABS

STEVIA TABS WITH NATURAL STEVIOL GLYKOSIDES

Stevia tabs are made from natural steviol glycosides and are made for daily consumption. The stevia plant is characterised by its strong natural sweetness. Stevia is ideal for sweetening food, drinks and desserts. Stevia tabs are an excellent supplement and natural nutritional support.

Stevia tabs are ideal for hot drinks such as coffee, tea or other hot beverages, as they dissolve quickly and provide a natural sweetness.

Stevia naturally contains fewer calories than sugar and is 200 times sweeter. Thanks to its high concentration, just 1-2 tabs per meal or portion are sufficient; for meals such as oatmeal, cereal or yoghurt, we recommend first dissolving the tablets in a small amount of hot water and then adding the sweetened liquid. As stevia does not affect blood sugar levels, it is insulin neutral and is ideal for diabetics.

ADVANTAGES

  • Free from lactose, maltodextrin and synthetic sweeteners
  • Naturally gluten-free
  • Low in calories and suitable for diabetics
  • Manufactured in Germany in compliance with EU regulations.

INGREDIENTS

Sodium bicarbonate, sweetener: steviol glycosides rebaudioside A97 (25%),

sodium citrate, solubiliser: leucine, magnesium salts of fatty acids.

CONTENTS

300 Tabs, 18 gr net in a dispenser, one tablet weighs 0.06 g.
