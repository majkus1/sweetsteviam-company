Wholesale Natural Sweeteners Supplier – SWEETSTEVIAM

Welcome to SWEETSTEVIAM – a leading wholesale supplier of natural sugar-free sweeteners in Europe. We specialize in providing high-quality stevia, erythritol, xylitol, sucralose, and other natural sweetening substances for food manufacturers, beverage producers, catering companies, and individual customers across Europe and worldwide.

Why Choose Natural Sugar-Free Sweeteners?

Natural sweeteners are a healthy alternative to traditional sugar. Our products, such as stevia (E960), erythritol, and xylitol, are ideal for keto diet followers, diabetics, and anyone who wants to reduce sugar intake without sacrificing sweet taste. All our sugar-free sweeteners are EU-compliant and have appropriate quality certificates.

Our Product Range – Natural Sweeteners Wholesale

In our wholesale catalog, you'll find a wide selection of natural sweeteners:

  • Stevia Rebaudiana (E960) – the sweetest natural sweetener, ideal for diabetics and keto diet followers
  • Erythritol – zero-calorie sweetener, safe for teeth
  • Xylitol – natural birch-derived sweetener with health benefits
  • Sucralose – intense sweetener, stable at high temperatures
  • Sorbitol – natural low-calorie sweetener and humectant
  • Stevia tablets – convenient form of stevia for sweetening beverages
