Form: Powder | Liquid | Tablets

Our Stevia Rebaudiana Extract (E960) is a premium, plant-derived sweetener with high Rebaudioside A content – the most pleasant-tasting steviol glycoside. Offered in powder, liquid, and tablet forms, it’s ideal for health-focused food production and personal use.

Key Benefits:

  • 100% pure Stevia extract
  • Zero calories, sugar-free
  • Contains Rebaudioside A – the sweetest steviol glycoside
  • Vegan, non-GMO, diabetic-friendly
  • Compliant with EU Regulation (E960)

Applications

  • Sweetening beverages: tea, juices, energy drinks
  • Low-sugar desserts and bakery
  • Nutritional supplements and health foods
  • Ideal for diabetic and keto product lines

2. Maltitol (E965) – Sugar-Free Bulk Sweetener

Form: Powder or Syrup

A versatile sweetener used in reduced-sugar candies and baked goods. It provides a sugar-like texture and mild sweetness.

Key Benefits:

  • Sugar-free and low glycemic
  • Moisture-retaining for confectionery
  • Non-cariogenic (tooth-friendly)
  • Vegan and EU-compliant (E965)

Applications

  • Chewing gum, candies, cakes
  • Low-sugar ice creams
  • Protein bars and cookies

Allergen-Free (EU Annex II Compliant)

  • Non-GMO and tooth-friendly
  • Perfect for diabetics and ketogenic diets
  • EU-approved (E968)

Applications

  • Tabletop sweeteners

Allergen-Free | Vegan | Clean-label

4. Xylitol (E967) – Dental-Friendly Sweetener

Form: Crystalline powder

Xylitol is a naturally occurring polyol known for its dental benefits and sweetness equal to sugar.

Key Benefits:

  • Prevents cavities and plaque formation
  • Suitable for diabetics (low glycemic)
  • EU food additive E967 compliant
  • Vegan, allergen-free

Applications

  • Chewing gum, lozenges
  • Toothpaste and oral care
  • Baked goods and sugar-free candy

5. Sucralose (E955) – High-Intensity Sweetener

Form: Powder

A powerful non-caloric sweetener, 600x sweeter than sugar. Highly stable at high temperatures.

Key Benefits:

  • Intense sweetness with no aftertaste
  • Calorie-free and heat-stable
  • Suitable for diabetics and low-sugar diets
  • Approved for food use across the EU (E955)

Applications

  • Beverages and flavored waters
  • Diet foods and supplements
  • Baking and syrups

6. Sorbitol (E420) – Moisture Retaining Agent

Form: Powder or Syrup

Sorbitol serves both as a sweetener and humectant, ideal for maintaining product freshness.

Key Benefits:

  • Mild sweetness (60% of sugar)
  • Moisture control in confections
  • Tooth-friendly and vegan
  • Complies with EU food law (E420)

Applications

  • Chewing gum, jellies
  • Low-sugar baked goods
  • Syrups, pharmaceuticals
